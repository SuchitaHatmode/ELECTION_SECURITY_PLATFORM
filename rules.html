Rules and Regulations

Please adhere to the following rules and regulations while voting:

  • Face should be clearly visible during the voting process.
  • Do not attempt to manipulate or influence others' votes.
  • Voting must be done individually, without external interference.
  • Respect the privacy and choices of other voters.
  • Ensure your voter ID and Aadhar number are accurate and verified.
  • Once a vote is cast, it cannot be altered or reversed.
  • Follow all instructions provided during the voting process.
  • Avoid any kind of misconduct or inappropriate behavior at the voting site.
